Privacy Policy

Introduction

SneakyBird Apps ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes our practices for collecting, using, and sharing information when you use our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of diagnostic information to improve our services:

  • Crash data
  • Performance data
  • Product interaction data
  • Other diagnostic data

This information is collected solely for app functionality and analytics purposes. The data is not linked to user identity and is not used for tracking.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Ensuring optimal app performance and stability
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Understanding app behavior patterns to improve user experience
  • Monitoring and enhancing system performance
  • Guiding future development and feature improvements

Sign in with Apple

For apps that support Sign in with Apple, we receive a unique, app-specific user identifier to maintain your account. This identifier is not linked to your personal information and is used solely for authentication purposes. Not all of our apps require or support Sign in with Apple - please refer to each app's individual features and settings for specific authentication methods available.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your diagnostic information with third parties. Any collected data is used exclusively for improving our applications' functionality and user experience.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
