Privacy Policy

Introduction

SneakyBird Apps ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes our practices for collecting, using, and sharing information when you use our mobile applications on both iOS and Android platforms.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of diagnostic information to improve our services across iOS and Android platforms:

  • Crash data: Information about app crashes to improve stability
  • Performance data: Metrics about app performance and responsiveness
  • Product interaction data: How users interact with app features
  • Other diagnostic data: Technical information to ensure proper functionality

This information is collected solely for app functionality and analytics purposes. The data is not linked to user identity and is not used for tracking. We comply with both Google Play's User Data policy and Apple's App Store guidelines regarding privacy and data collection.

App Permissions

Depending on the specific app and platform, we may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access: For sending diagnostic and analytics data
  • Storage access: For saving app preferences and data locally on your device
  • Other permissions specific to each app's functionality will be requested explicitly when needed

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Ensuring optimal app performance and stability
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Understanding app behavior patterns to improve user experience
  • Monitoring and enhancing system performance
  • Guiding future development and feature improvements

Authentication Methods

Some of our apps may support various sign-in methods including Sign in with Apple (iOS) and Google Sign-In (Android). When you use these authentication services, we receive a unique, app-specific user identifier to maintain your account. This identifier is not linked to your personal information and is used solely for authentication purposes. Not all of our apps require or support sign-in - please refer to each app's individual features and settings for specific authentication methods available.

Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. Most of your app data is stored locally on your device. Any data transmitted to our servers is encrypted during transmission and stored in secure cloud environments.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of data collection where applicable
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your diagnostic information with third parties. Any collected data is used exclusively for improving our applications' functionality and user experience. We may share anonymous, aggregated statistics about app usage to describe our services to partners or the public.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
